# Cost Analysis

## AWS Services Used

### Amazon S3
Purpose:
- Store static website files.

Estimated Cost:
- Free Tier eligible.
- Outside the Free Tier, costs are minimal for a small portfolio website.

---

### Amazon CloudFront

Purpose:
- Deliver website globally.
- Enable HTTPS.
- Improve performance through caching.

Estimated Cost:
- Covered by the AWS Free Tier for this project.
- Low cost for light traffic after the Free Tier.

---

### AWS IAM

Purpose:
- Secure account access.
- Manage permissions.

Estimated Cost:
- Free.

---

## Estimated Monthly Cost

For a personal portfolio with low traffic:

Approximately **$0** while within the AWS Free Tier.

After the Free Tier, costs remain very low for a portfolio with light usage.
